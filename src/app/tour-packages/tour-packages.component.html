Attractions

Sikkim, nestled in the Himalayas, is a haven for adventure and nature enthusiasts. The state offers thrilling activities like trekking, river rafting, and hill cycling. Paragliding over stunning landscapes provides an unforgettable aerial view, while the annual flower shows showcase a vibrant array of blooms, including rare orchids and rhododendrons. Wildlife enthusiasts can explore rich biodiversity in its sanctuaries, and serene monasteries offer a glimpse into Sikkim's spiritual heritage. With its unique blend of adventure, natural beauty, and cultural richness, Sikkim promises an unforgettable experience for every traveler.
