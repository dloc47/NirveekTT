Cancellation policy for land package

Last updated: March 1, 2024

Please read these policy carefully before using Our Service.

  1. 1. Cancellation more than 30 days before the starting date: 5% of the total land package cost will be charged as cancellation fees.

  2. 2. Cancellation between 15 to 30 days before the starting date: 30% of the total land package cost will be charged as cancellation fees.

  3. 3. Cancellation between 0 to 14 days before the starting date: 50% of the total land package cost will be charged as cancellation fees.
